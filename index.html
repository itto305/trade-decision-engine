// the framework
5 CRITERIA.
ONE VERDICT.

Every trade gets scored across five non-negotiable checkpoints. All 5 must pass before you enter — no exceptions.

01
🎯
Risk / Reward
Minimum 2:1 ratio required. If the math doesn't work, the trade doesn't work.
02
🛡️
Loss Tolerance
Max drawdown vs portfolio size. Keeps a single trade from damaging your book.
03
⚡
Your Edge
Why will you win this trade? Without a defined edge, you're gambling — not trading.
04
🌐
Diversification
Portfolio correlation check. Even the best traders are wrong 30–40% of the time.
05
📐
Position Sizing
Kelly Criterion-based optimal size. Never bet more than your edge justifies.
